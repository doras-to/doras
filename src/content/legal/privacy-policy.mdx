## Privacy Policy for Doras.to

Thank you for using Doras.to ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

1. Information We Collect

a. Account Information: When you sign up for an account on Doras.to, we collect your email address and password. If you choose to sign in through a third-party Single Sign-On (SSO) provider, such as Supabase, we may collect necessary information as provided by the SSO provider.

b. Social Media Integration: In order to provide certain features like link in bio and media kit pages, we may request access to your social media accounts. We use this information to pull data from social media APIs, including but not limited to follows, likes, and other engagement metrics.

2. How We Use Your Information

a. Account Management: We use your account information to create and manage your Doras.to account.

b. Social Media Data: The data retrieved from social media platforms is used to enhance your profile and provide you with insights into your social media presence on our platform.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Compliance)

a. Consent: By using Doras.to, you consent to the processing of your personal data as outlined in this Privacy Policy.

b. Legitimate Interests: We may process your personal data for our legitimate interests, such as providing and improving our services. You have the right to object to such processing.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

You can review and update your account information at any time. You may also disconnect your social media accounts from Doras.to at your discretion.

6. Children's Privacy

Doras.to is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to request deletion.

7. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside Ireland, your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA). By using Doras.to, you consent to such international transfer.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at legal@doras.to.
